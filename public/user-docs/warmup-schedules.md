# Warm-up Schedules

Account burning is expensive. The platform includes an auto-advancing Warm-up sequence designed to build trust profiles for imported accounts slowly over time.

## Warm-up Stages

1. **Stage 1 (Days 1-3)**: Discovery phase. The account scrolls the feed and watches videos to build session metrics. Likes are disabled.
2. **Stage 2 (Days 4-7)**: Micro-engagement. The account begins to drop 1-2 likes per session.
3. **Stage 3 (Days 8-14)**: Trust building. The daily action limit is raised to 15. The account can follow profiles.
4. **Stage 4 (Full Speed)**: Graduated. The account can participate in aggressive hashtag engagement strategies and has a max daily budget of 150 actions.

## Auto-Advancement

If an account goes 48 hours without experiencing a soft block, the platform will automatically prompt you in the **Social Dashboard** to graduate it to the next tier.
